About Our Map

Powered by Open Data

To provide the most accurate and accessible navigation experience, our interactive campus map is built using publicly available geospatial data. By leveraging open-source mapping resources like OpenStreetMap alongside community-driven datasets, we have meticulously charted Carleton’s buildings, pedestrian pathways, parking facilities, and transit networks. This project not only makes it easier for students, staff, and visitors to explore our beautiful riverside campus, but also reflects our commitment to collaborative innovation and accessible technology.
